About

Welcome to d'Mari. Where style meets conversation. Inspired by the vibrant culture of the Philippines, d'Mari takes its name from the word "Mari", short for Marites. A play on the gossiping arts that creep through the cracks of everyday life. Just like a well-told story, every piece in our collection is designed to catch your eyes and stir up some conversation. Our slogan, "The gossip that suits your style", says it all about d'Mari, it is not just making headlines but also conversation. Check out our new collections and become part of the talk of the town.
